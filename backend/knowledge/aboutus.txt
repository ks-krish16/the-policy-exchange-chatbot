The Policy Exchange Knowledge Base

ABOUT
The Policy Exchange is an InsurTech platform under Fairvalue Insuretech Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2022 by industry veterans with over 50 years of combined BFSI experience.

MISSION
To improve liquidity in life insurance policies by connecting financially distressed policyholders with investors while ensuring insurance protection continues.

PROBLEM
Many policyholders surrender or discontinue their life insurance policies due to financial difficulties. This causes:
- Loss of insurance protection for policyholders
- Lower persistency for insurance companies
- Loss of renewal income for insurance agents

SOLUTION
The Policy Exchange provides a marketplace where:
- Policyholders can assign their policy to investors.
- Investors pay the surrender value and future premiums.
- Investors receive maturity benefits.
- Policyholders continue to receive life insurance protection.
- Insurance companies improve persistency.
- Insurance agents continue receiving renewal commissions.

BENEFITS

Policyholder
- Immediate liquidity
- Continued life insurance coverage

Investor
- Stable investment opportunity
- Guaranteed maturity benefits

Insurance Company
- Higher policy persistency
- Reduced policy lapses

Insurance Agent
- Continued renewal income

INDUSTRY IMPACT
Current surrender/lapse value is approximately ₹1.5 lakh crore with around 15% CAGR.
